Note: the code in this folder (SST/_mambaformer) is mambaformer, rather than SST. Mambaformer paper is old and short version of the SST (latest and long) paper. In SST paper, mambaformer is a ablated baseline.

Mambaformer paper (old and short version): "Integrating Mamba and Transformer for Long-Short Range Time Series Forecasting".
SST paper (latest and long version): "SST: Multi-Scale Hybrid Mamba-Transformer Experts for Long-Short Range Time Series Forecasting".
